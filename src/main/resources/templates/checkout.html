Rides

Super Slide

This slide has three big lanes and is 20m long, 5m wide and is great fun for all ages.

Rockwall

The rockwall provides great entertainment for all ages! With safety harnesses to allow the children to safely come back down to the ground after scaling the 9.5m rock face, what fun!

Ferris Wheel

Two fire engines, two corvettes, two dune buggies, too much fun! Great for fundraising events with getting through the line-ups fast.

Speed Racer

What carnival would be complete without this great showground tradition? Children love it!

Bow Wow Castle

Scooby Doo eat your heart out. This little beauty is perfect for the younger age groups, and a great addition close to a larger castle for the little ones to get their confidence up. Perfect on its own as well

Sports Castle

This is big, it’s very big and great exercise. An awesome way to burn up a bit of that excessive energy. Our Sports Castle is extremely popular at both fetes and sporting breakups. It’s made up of one big slide, and 2 jumping areas on each side.

Animal Farm

What child doesn’t love animals? We bring along 30 plus, hand raised farm animals for the children to hold and pet. The pen is 5m x 6m which is full of sheep, lambs, goats, kids, calves, ducks, ducklings, chickens, chicks and guinea pigs.

Pony Rides

The nature and temperament of our ponies is hard to match. We bring along the required number of ponies, with its own wrangler, western saddle, child’s helmet and all the necessary equipment required to ensure the safety of our customers.
